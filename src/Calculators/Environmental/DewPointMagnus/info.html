The following calculator helps you works out the component values to design a low-pass, single-stage, passive RC filter. The cut-off frequency, \( f_c \), is given by:

$$ f_c = \frac{1}{2\pi RC} $$

where:
\( f_c \) = the cutoff frequency of the low-pass RC filter (a.k.a knee frequency, -3dB point)
\( R \) = resistance of the resistor
\( C \) = capacitance of the capacitor
